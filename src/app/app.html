Vision

Led by the good Shepherd we surrender ourselves to produce many seeds of wheat throughout communities by rooting young adults in God's word and equipping them with how to reset to remain in Jesus' love.

Mission

Guided by the good Shepherd, we partner with families and communities to plant the seeds of faith in young hearts - rooting young adults in God's Word, teaching them how to remain in Jesus' love, and equipping them to grow and thrive as disciples who multiply His light in the world.
