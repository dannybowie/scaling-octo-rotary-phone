Prodigals United

Vision

Led by the good Shepherd we surrender ourselves to produce many seeds of wheat throughout communities by rooting kids in God's word and equipping them with how to reset to remain in Jesus' love.

Mission

Guided by the good Shepherd, we partner with families and communities to plant the seeds of faith in young hearts - rooting children in God's Word, teaching them how to remain in Jesus' love, and equipping them to grow and thrive as disiples who multiply his light in the world.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our actions.
  • Compassion: We are dedicated to understanding and addressing the needs of those we serve.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of working together to achieve common goals.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and seek new ways to solve challenges.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do.

Objectives

  1. To provide educational programs that enhance skills and knowledge.
  2. To create support networks that foster community engagement.
  3. To advocate for policies that promote social justice and equality.
  4. To develop partnerships with organizations that share our vision and values.
  5. To continuously evaluate and improve our programs and services.
